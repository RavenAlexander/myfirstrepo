How to Make a Sandwich:

  1. Get bread, peanut butter and jelly.
  2. Take two slices of bread, separate them
  3. Use knife to spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  4. Use knife to spread jelly on other slice of bread
  5. Bring both slices together so the peanut butter and jelly are touching
  6. Voila!
